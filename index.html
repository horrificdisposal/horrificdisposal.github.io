About

Horrific Disposal, an Amsterdam-based death metal band, formed in 2023 and released their debut demo, "Murder in Basement." In 2024, the band underwent a significant lineup evolution, resulting in a truly international group with members from Brazil, UK, the Netherlands, and Turkey. They have recently unleashed "Revenge Abduction," which showcases their new, intensely brutal style. New releases coming soon.
